## Objection Handling Guide

### "We already have retry logic"
**Response**: "Great! API Sentinel adds multi-gateway intelligence. Your retry logic retries the same failing gateway. We automatically switch to a working gateway, which is why we recover 80% vs typical 20-30% with basic retries."

### "What if both gateways fail?"
**Response**: "Then you're in the same situation as today - we return a user-friendly error. But 80% of the time, at least one gateway works, so you recover revenue you'd otherwise lose."

### "Security and PCI compliance?"
**Response**: "We never touch payment data - we only handle routing decisions. Your existing PCI compliance covers our integration. We just direct traffic to your already-compliant gateways."

### "What's the latency impact?"
**Response**: "Zero on successful transactions. On failures, we add 1-2 seconds to recover vs losing the sale entirely. Most customers find 2 seconds acceptable vs a completely failed transaction."

### "Pricing concerns"
**Response**: "Fair question. For your volume of $[X]/month, typical failure rate is 3% = $[Y] lost. We recover 80% = $[Z]. Our cost is $[fraction of Z]. Clear positive ROI. Plus 30-day money-back guarantee if you don't see 10x return."

### "Integration complexity"
**Response**: "30 minutes for most teams. It's a 2-line code change to wrap your existing payment calls. We have Flutter, React, Node.js SDKs ready. I can pair-program the integration with your team if helpful."
